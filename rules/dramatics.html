Singing (Solo) — Rules

  • There will be 2 rounds.
  • Mashups are not allowed.
  • Maximum time per performance: 7 minutes.
  • No electrical instruments will be entertained.
  • Participants may perform any song of their choice (Eastern or Western, originals or covers).
  • Participants may bring one additional musician/instrumentalist.
  • Participants are not allowed to repeat the same song in subsequent rounds.

Drama — Rules and Guidelines

  • Open theme will be followed. Judges reserve the right to remove or disqualify the team on the spot.
  • Each play: maximum of 30 minutes.
  • Language: English, Urdu or Punjabi.
  • Team size: maximum of 12 participants.
  • Script must be submitted prior to the due date.
  • Props: teams must bring their own props.
  • No flame of any type permitted, including candles.
  • No real weapons of any kind (e.g., sword, knife, etc.).
  • Teams are responsible for their own makeup and costumes.
  • Teams are responsible for their own set pieces. No part of any set may be attached to any part of the stage, curtains, draperies, or other existing equipment of the host site.
  • Team Qissa will provide basic stage lights and sound equipment.
  • Reporting: teams must show up at the mentioned time; otherwise, they will be disqualified.
  • Judges' decisions are final and shall not be disputed.
  • There will be a single round in this category.
  • No vulgar dialogues, actions, or dance steps are allowed.

Note

All participants are required to set the stage prior to their specific performance time and must remove their belongings after their performance. In case of any missing item, NUML Dramatic Team will not be responsible. The event host may change rules according to the situation. Judges' decisions are final and shall not be disputed.

Monologue — Guidelines

  • Duration: 3 to 5 minutes.
  • Choice: recreate a monologue from any film/TV series OR perform an original monologue.
  • Original scripts will be awarded extra marks.
  • Submit the monologue script via email at least three days before performance.
  • Props are allowed; performers may bring their own.
  • Content restrictions: no profanity, vulgarity, or blasphemous content.
