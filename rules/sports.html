Futsal (Male Only) — Rules and Guidelines

  • Played by 5 players on each side.
  • Each team may bring 3 substitutes (squad of 8).
  • Rolling substitutes only from the squad.
  • Direct free kicks and throw-ins are not allowed.
  • Fouls or misconduct can result in yellow or red cards.
  • 5 fouls in a single half lead to a penalty kick.
  • Abusive language results in a straight red card.
  • 3 minutes suspension for a player followed by red card.
  • No player may play for a second team.
  • Referee's decision is final.
  • Management reserves the right to admit and make changes in rules.

Ludo — Rules and Guidelines

  • Each player rolls a die; highest roller begins. Turns proceed clockwise.
  • To enter a token from yard to starting square, a player must roll a six.
  • On rolling a six, a player may draw a token from home (if available) or move a piece six squares.
