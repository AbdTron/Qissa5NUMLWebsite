Qirat — Rules and Guidelines

  • General category for both Male and Female.
  • Each delegation may send more than one participant (Male or Female).
  • Qari can recite any verses of the Holy Quran of their own choice.
  • Time: 3 to 4 minutes per participant.
  • Participants must have sound knowledge of Tajweed and recite with Tarteel.
  • Repetition of verses is not allowed.
  • Judges' decision is final.

Naat — Rules and Guidelines

  • Separate category for Male and Female.
  • Naat must be in Urdu.
  • Each delegation may send more than one participant (Male or Female).
  • Selected Naat must not be on the tune of any song.
  • Time: 3 to 5 minutes per participant. Exceeding time may incur penalties.
  • Paper reading is not allowed.
  • Judging based on voice, recitation, and selection of kalaam.
  • Judges' decision is final.
