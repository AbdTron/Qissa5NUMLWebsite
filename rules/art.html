Painting

Themes

  • Shadows of the mind
  • Time as a canvas
  • Nature's last cry
  • Through the eyes of a child in Gaza

Rules

  • A3 canvas sheets will be provided.
  • Contestants must bring their own material: acrylic paints, brushes, palette.
  • The artwork must be the contestant's original hand‑drawn creation.
  • Participants may view a reference picture already saved on their phones; on‑spot googling is not allowed.
  • Time limit: 2 hours 30 minutes.

Judgment Criteria

  1. Interpretation and clarity of the theme.
  2. Creativity, uniqueness, and originality of the depicted theme.
  3. Quality of artistic composition and overall design.
  4. Command of the medium used and technical skill.
