Literature — General Guidelines

Applies to all Literature modules (English & Urdu).

All Categories Rulebook PDF

Guidelines

  • Originality of content is essential.
  • Maintain objectivity and avoid bias.
  • Plagiarism and AI-generated content, in any form, will not be tolerated.
  • The judges' decision will be final.
  • Repetition of lines having the same meaning is discouraged.

Note

Online submissions must be in MS Word or PDF using the format “Times New Roman”, font size 12. The participant must include their name and university affiliation on the document.

One student can participate in multiple modules, but only one entry per module is allowed.

Registration via Google Forms is required prior to submission.
