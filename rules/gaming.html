Tekken 8

Team Rules

  • Players may bring their own controllers.
  • Society-provided controllers will also be available.
  • Players arriving more than 5 minutes late will be disqualified; opponent advances.

Game Rules

  • Qualifiers: Best of 3 (each game 3 rounds of 60 seconds).
  • Semi-finals and Finals: Best of 5.
  • DLC characters/content are not allowed.
  • Random stage select for all games. If players pick a stage, the match restarts.
  • Winners cannot change characters; losers may change characters.
  • Any game-breaking glitch causing delay results in a loss; repeated abuse leads to disqualification.
  • Each match: 3 rounds, 60 seconds per round.
  • After losing, a player may change either the stage or the character — not both.
  • No pausing mid-match.
  • Toss decides side selection. Combo assist is prohibited.
  • Players absent when called will be disqualified.
  • Management resolves issues; their decision is final.

FIFA

Rules

  • Open to both males and females; no separate categories.
  • Group stage followed by knockout stage.
  • All participants must be at the venue 30 minutes before the game starts.
  • Fixture schedule will be communicated in advance.
  • Foul language or threats against the game admin lead to disqualification.
  • No-show results in a 1–0 walkover to the opponent.
  • Match duration: 4 minutes per half; Game speed: Normal; Difficulty: Legendary.
  • Club and country teams are allowed.
  • Each match is between two players.
