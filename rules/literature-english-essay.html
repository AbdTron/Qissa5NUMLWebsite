Rules and Guidelines

  • Word limit: 1800–2000 words.
  • Submission format: MS Word or PDF, “Times New Roman”, font size 12.
  • The theme must align with the given topic.
  • No objectionable remarks against any religious or political school of thought (straight disqualification).
  • Plagiarism will immediately disqualify the entry.
  • Quotes/references must be properly cited; they carry marks.
  • The decision of the judges will be final.
